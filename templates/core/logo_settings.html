How to Install on Mobile / Desktop

Staff can install this portal as an app on their phone or computer — no app store needed.

Android (Chrome)
  1. Open the portal in Chrome
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right)
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  4. Tap "Add" — icon appears on home screen
iPhone / iPad (Safari)
  1. Open the portal in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add"
Desktop (Chrome / Edge)
  1. Open the portal in Chrome or Edge
  2. Click the install icon in the address bar
  3. Click "Install"
  4. The portal opens in its own window like a native app
